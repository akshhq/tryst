About TRYST

TRYST is Keshav Mahavidyalaya’s annual technical and cultural extravaganza, growing into a vibrant multi-day celebration that attracts students and participants from across Delhi and beyond.

The festival features 20+ competitions, engaging workshops, cultural performances, and electrifying musical acts that transform the Keshav Mahavidyalaya campus into an immersive universe of creativity, art, and energy.
