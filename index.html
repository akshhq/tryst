Curator's Statement

The Digital Gallery
of Tomorrow

TRYST is the annual cultural extravaganza of Keshav Mahavidyalaya, Delhi University. It brings together students from across the country to showcase their talents in music, dance, art, theatre, and more.

This year, we invite you to step into a curated fusion of high-art aesthetics and digital fantasy — bridging the classical and the boundless imagination of the future.
